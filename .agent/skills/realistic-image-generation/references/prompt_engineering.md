# Prompt Engineering for Photorealism

## Principles

1.  **Nano Banana 2 (Gemini 3.1 Flash) Power**: Utilize the model's new capabilities for rapid iteration and pro-level scene planning.
2.  **Textual Precision**: Nano Banana 2 excels at legible text. Specify exact text, font style, and placement (e.g., "with the word 'FLEXPRESS' in bold sans-serif font on the side of the truck").
3.  **Extreme Resolution & Lighting**: Take advantage of native 2K/4K resolution and physics-accurate lighting. Specify resolution clearly.
4.  **Technical Depth**: Continue specifying camera gear (e.g., "35mm lens", "f/1.8") and atmospheric effects to guide the model's enhanced reasoning.
5.  **Subject Authenticity**: Describe subjects with precision to leverage the model's improved character consistency.

## Prompt Structure Template

`[Subject Description] + [Action/Pose] + [Setting/Background] + [Text Requirements] + [Lighting & Atmosphere] + [Resolution & Technical Quality]`

"A hyper-realistic close-up of a premium electric delivery van driving through a rain-slicked neon street at night. On the side of the van, the text 'FLEXPRESS' is clearly rendered in a glowing cyan futuristic font. Physics-accurate reflections of neon signs on the van's polished metallic surface. Shot on Sony A7R IV, 35mm f/1.8 lens, sharp focus on the logo, cinematic lighting with realistic rain particles, 4K resolution, ultra-detailed textures."
